What Privilege does

You want AI help on a confidential document, but you can't paste it into ChatGPT or Claude. Privilege masks the sensitive parts on your machine, then has GPT-5.6 attack the masked version to check whether the client is still identifiable. If it is, Privilege tightens it further. You get back a safe document to use with any AI, and restore the real names locally when the answer comes back.

  1. Say what must stay confidential (once per client).
  2. Add a document.
  3. Get a verified-safe version to paste into any AI tool.
  4. Paste the AI's reply back to restore the real names.
