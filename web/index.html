What Privilege does

You want AI help on a confidential PDF, but you can't upload it as-is. Privilege masks the names and terms you declare, has GPT-5.6 attack the result against your engagement rules, and revises material matches. The result is scoped to that policy: undeclared names or facts may remain.

  1. Say what must stay confidential (once per client).
  2. Upload the client PDF.
  3. Get a PDF checked against that engagement policy — revise if the attacker found a material match.
  4. Paste the AI's reply back to restore the real names.
